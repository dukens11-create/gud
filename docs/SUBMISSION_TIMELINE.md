## Contingency Planning

### If Rejected by Google Play

**Timeline Impact:** +3-5 days

**Steps:**
1. Read rejection reason carefully
2. Fix the specific issue
3. Update documentation if needed
4. Resubmit within 24 hours
5. Usually approved within 1-2 days

**Common Issues:**
- Policy violations (update description)
- Crashes (fix and resubmit)
- Privacy policy (clarify data usage)

---

### If Rejected by Apple

**Timeline Impact:** +2-4 days

**Steps:**
1. Read rejection reason in Resolution Center
2. Fix the specific issue
3. Update metadata if needed
4. Resubmit with explanation
5. Usually approved within 24 hours

**Common Issues:**
- Guideline 2.1 (fix crashes)
- Guideline 4.0 (improve UI)
- Guideline 5.1 (clarify privacy)
- Test account issues

**Expedited Review:**
- Available for critical bugs
- Request through App Store Connect
- Usually approved same day

---

### If Both Platforms Approved at Different Times

**Strategy:** Coordinate launch

**Option A: Wait for Both**
- Launch simultaneously on both platforms
- Unified marketing campaign
- Better user experience

**Option B: Staggered Launch**
- Launch on first approved platform
- Announce "Available on X, coming soon to Y"
- Launch second platform when ready

**Recommendation:** Wait for both (max 1 week delay)
