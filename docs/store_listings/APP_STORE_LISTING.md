## Description

*Maximum 4000 characters (Current: ~3980)*

**Transform Your Trucking Operations with GUD Express**

GUD Express is the comprehensive mobile solution designed specifically for trucking companies, dispatchers, and professional drivers. Whether you're managing a fleet or driving cross-country, GUD Express streamlines every aspect of your logistics workflow with powerful features and an intuitive interface.

**FOR DISPATCHERS & ADMINISTRATORS**

**Load Management Made Simple**
Create, assign, and track deliveries with just a few taps. Add detailed pickup and delivery information, assign loads to available drivers instantly, and monitor all shipments in real-time. Powerful search and filtering help you find exactly what you need.

**Complete Fleet Control**
Manage your entire driver fleet from one central dashboard. View driver profiles, track performance metrics, monitor real-time locations during active trips, and access comprehensive earnings and payment history for every driver.

**Advanced Business Analytics**
Make informed decisions with comprehensive dashboards displaying key performance indicators. Track revenue and expenses, analyze performance trends, and generate custom reports for any date range. Export data to CSV for deeper analysis or create professional PDF invoices automatically.

**Professional Documentation**
Generate detailed load reports, export comprehensive data for accounting, create professional invoices, and maintain a complete archive of all proof of delivery documents. Everything you need for business operations and compliance.

**FOR PROFESSIONAL DRIVERS**

**Streamlined Trip Management**
Access all assigned loads in one convenient location. Get complete pickup and delivery information including addresses, contact details, and special instructions. Start and complete trips with a single tap, and track your progress in real-time.

**Smart GPS Features**
Share your location with dispatchers during active trips (only when you choose). Manual location updates give you control over your privacy. Automatic route tracking calculates accurate mileage, and complete location history is available for all completed deliveries.

**Digital Proof of Delivery**
Go completely paperless with digital POD capture. Use your camera to photograph delivery confirmations, add detailed notes and timestamps, and upload instantly to secure cloud storage. Never lose a delivery receipt again, and access your complete POD history anytime.

**Real-Time Earnings Tracking**
Always know your income with real-time earnings calculation. View detailed breakdowns for each load, access comprehensive historical reports, and easily distinguish between paid and unpaid loads. Take control of your financial planning.

**Comprehensive Expense Management**
Track fuel, tolls, meals, and all other expenses. Attach receipt photos for documentation, benefit from automatic categorization, and generate detailed expense reports for reimbursement or tax purposes.

### Testing Instructions for App Review

**To Test Admin Features:**
1. Log in with admin credentials
2. View dashboard showing all loads
3. Create a new load (pre-filled data available)
4. Assign load to test driver
5. View driver list and statistics
6. Generate a report or export data

**To Test Driver Features:**
1. Log out and log in with driver credentials
2. View assigned loads on driver dashboard
3. Open a load in "Pending" status
4. Start the trip
5. Share location (allow location permission)
6. Complete trip and upload POD photo
7. View earnings in statistics

### Notes for Reviewer

**Location Services:**
Location is requested only when:
- User is a driver
- User has an active trip in progress
- Always with user permission

Location is NEVER tracked:
- When app is in background
- For admin users
- When driver has no active trips

**Camera Access:**
Camera is used only for:
- Proof of delivery photo capture
- Expense receipt photos
- Always initiated by user action

**Firebase Integration:**
The app uses Firebase for:
- Authentication
- Real-time database (Firestore)
- Cloud storage for photos
- Analytics and crash reporting
All Firebase services are configured for production use.

## Additional Requirements

### App Store Distribution Certificate

Required for production builds:
- Apple Developer Program membership ($99/year)
- Distribution certificate generated in Apple Developer account
- App signed with distribution profile
- Uploaded via Xcode or Application Loader

### App Transport Security (ATS)

**Configuration:**
```xml
<key>NSAppTransportSecurity</key>
<dict>
    <key>NSAllowsArbitraryLoads</key>
    <false/>
</dict>
```

Firebase uses HTTPS, so no ATS exceptions needed.

**Required Privacy Declarations:**
