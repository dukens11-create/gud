# Privacy Policy for GUD Express

**Effective Date:** February 2024  
**Last Updated:** February 6, 2026

GUD Express ("we," "our," or "us") operates the GUD Express mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide to us when creating an account and using the App:

**Account Information:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Password (encrypted and securely stored)
- User role (Administrator or Driver)

**Driver-Specific Information:**
- Truck number/identifier
- Commercial driver's license information
- Employment status and history
- Performance metrics and ratings

**Load and Delivery Data:**
- Load assignments and details
- Delivery confirmations and timestamps
- Proof of delivery photos
- Delivery notes and comments
- Trip history and records

**Financial Information:**
- Earnings records
- Payment history
- Expense reports and receipts
- Invoice data

### 1.2 Location Information

**GPS Location Data:**
- Real-time GPS coordinates during active trips
- Location history for completed deliveries
- Route information and mileage tracking
- Manual location sharing by drivers

We collect location data **only when:**
- You are actively logged in as a driver
- You have an active/in-progress trip
- You manually share your location
- You have granted location permissions to the app

Location tracking can be disabled at any time through your device settings, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of the App.

### 1.3 Photos and Camera Access

We collect photos when you:
- Upload proof of delivery (POD) images
- Capture load documentation
- Submit expense receipts

Camera access is requested only when you initiate photo capture within the app. Photos are stored securely in Firebase Cloud Storage and associated with the relevant load or expense record.

### 1.4 Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information:**
- Device type, model, and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- App version

**Usage Data:**
- Features and screens accessed
- Time spent in the app
- Button clicks and interactions
- Search queries within the app
- Error logs and crash reports

### 2.4 Legal and Security
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Protect user safety and security
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Resolve disputes

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Firebase Cloud Infrastructure

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

**Firebase Authentication:**
- Industry-standard encryption for passwords
- Secure token-based authentication
- Multi-factor authentication support (when enabled)

**Cloud Firestore:**
- Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
- Encrypted data at rest
- Role-based security rules
- Regular automated backups

**Firebase Cloud Storage:**
- Secure photo and document storage
- Access control and authentication
- Encrypted transmission and storage

**Firebase Crashlytics:**
- Crash and error reporting
- Anonymous device and usage data
- No personally identifiable information in crash logs

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement various security measures including:
- Data encryption in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and permissions
- Firewall protection
- Intrusion detection systems

### 3.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:
- Your account is active
- Required for service provision
- Necessary for legal, tax, or regulatory purposes

Typical retention periods:
- Active account data: Duration of account plus 30 days
- Transaction records: 7 years (for tax and legal compliance)
- Location data: 90 days from trip completion
- Photos/POD: 3 years from delivery date
- Crash logs: 90 days

## 7. GPS and Location Tracking Disclosure

### 7.1 When Location is Collected
Location data is collected:
- **During Active Trips:** When a driver starts a trip and until it is completed
- **Manual Sharing:** When a driver manually shares their location
- **Never in Background:** Location is NOT tracked when the app is closed or you're logged out

### 7.2 Purpose of Location Collection
We use location data to:
- Track delivery progress in real-time
- Calculate accurate mileage for trips
- Provide route information
- Verify delivery completion
- Generate location-based reports

### 7.3 Location Sharing Control
Drivers have full control:
- Location sharing only occurs during active trips
- Can disable location permissions at any time
- No location tracking for admin users
- All location data is viewable to admins during active trips

## 8. Photo and Camera Usage

### 8.1 Photo Collection
We access your camera and photo library only when you:
- Capture proof of delivery photos
- Upload load documentation
- Submit expense receipts

### 8.2 Photo Storage and Usage
- Photos are securely stored in Firebase Cloud Storage
- Only accessible to authorized users (admins and the driver who uploaded)
- Used solely for business purposes (delivery verification, record keeping)
- Not used for facial recognition or any other purpose
- Not shared with third parties except as required by law

### 8.3 Photo Deletion
Photos can be deleted:
- When the associated load is deleted
- Upon account deletion (after retention period)
- By request through our Data Deletion Policy

## 9. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The GUD Express app is **not intended for children under 13 years of age**.

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
