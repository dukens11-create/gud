# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2024

GUD Express ("we," "our," or "us") operates the GUD Express mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information:

**Account Information:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- User role (Admin or Driver)
- Account credentials (encrypted)

**Driver-Specific Information:**
- Truck number
- License information
- Employment status
- Performance metrics

**Location Data:**
- Real-time GPS coordinates
- Location history during trips
- Geofence entry/exit events
- Route information

**Transaction Data:**
- Load assignments
- Delivery confirmations
- Earnings records
- Expense reports

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

**Usage Data:**
- App features used
- Time spent in app
- Screen interactions
- Crash reports and errors

### 1.3 Photos and Camera

- Proof of delivery images
- Receipt photos for expenses
- Profile pictures

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

### 2.1 Service Delivery
- Managing user accounts
- Assigning and tracking loads
- Processing payments
- Facilitating communication between admins and drivers

### 2.2 Location Services
- Real-time driver tracking
- Route optimization
- Geofence monitoring for automatic status updates
- Navigation assistance

### 2.3 Analytics and Improvement
- Analyzing app usage patterns
- Identifying and fixing bugs
- Improving user experience
- Developing new features

### 2.4 Communication
- Sending load assignments
- Delivery notifications
- System updates and announcements
- Customer support

### 2.5 Legal and Security
- Preventing fraud
- Enforcing terms of service
- Complying with legal obligations
- Protecting user safety

## 3. Information Sharing

### 3.1 Within GUD Express

Your information is shared:
- Between authorized admins and drivers
- With dispatchers for load management
- With accounting for payment processing

### 3.2 Third-Party Services

We share information with:

**Firebase (Google):**
- Authentication services
- Database hosting
- Cloud storage
- Analytics
- Crash reporting

**Google Maps:**
- Location services
- Navigation
- Geocoding

**Payment Processors:**
- Stripe or similar (when integrated)

**Cloud Services:**
- AWS or Google Cloud for backups

### 3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information:
- To comply with legal obligations
- To respond to subpoenas or court orders
- To protect our rights and safety
- To prevent fraud or illegal activity

### 3.4 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest
- Secure authentication (Firebase Auth)
- Role-based access control
- Regular security audits
- Code obfuscation in releases

### 4.2 Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive accounts:** Data retained for 90 days after last activity
- **Deleted accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Legal requirements:** Some data retained longer for compliance

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of non-essential communications

### 5.2 Location Data Control

You can:
- Disable location services (may limit functionality)
- Control background location access
- View location history
- Delete location data

### 5.3 Marketing Communications

You can:
- Opt-out of promotional emails
- Manage notification preferences
- Unsubscribe from marketing messages

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

## 7. Location-Based Services

### 7.1 Background Location

We collect location data in the background for:
- Real-time driver tracking during trips
- Automatic load status updates
- Route history
- Geofence monitoring

### 7.2 Location Permission

You must grant location permission to use driver features. You can revoke permission in device settings, but this will limit app functionality.

### 7.3 Location Accuracy

We use GPS for high-accuracy location tracking. You can adjust accuracy in device settings.

### 8.2 Third-Party Tracking
